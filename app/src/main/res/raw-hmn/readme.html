AllTrans yuav zoo li cas

Nws tau ntau xyoo ntawv nyeem tag nrho hauv ib app ib hom lus los ntawm ib cov lus mus rau lwm tus thaum runtime.

Hais piv ib app yog German. Ib tug neeg twg yuav xaiv tus lub npe app, thiab cov lus yuav tsum conversion (tej say German rau lus Askiv).

Ces thaum twg tus neeg siv yuav siv tus cai app, tag nrho cov ntawv nyeem, ANYWHERE hauv app, yog los hloov cov lus Askiv kom muaj sib npaug.

Qhov no yog ib yam zoo rau qhov uas Google Translate ua haujlwm nyob rau hauv Chrome.

Ceeb toom - vim kev txwv, qhov no yuav tsis ua hauj lwm nrog ntau ua si. Tsis muaj dab tsi yuav ua tau txog qhov no.

Siv AllTrans

No thov kom koj mus zwm npe dawb rau ib qhov tseem ceeb los ntawm Microsoft lossis Yandex Translate. Saib cov lus qhia hauv qab no yuav ua li cas mus rau hauv lub lag luam.

Yandex Translate txhawb ntau hom lus, muaj cov tau ntau yooj yim signup neej, thiab yuav tsum tsis muaj CREDIT CARD.

Microsoft Translate num zoo translations, tiam sis yuav tsum tau ib daim credit card mus zwm npe. (Tiam sis tsis txhob poob siab, tsis muaj dab tsi yuav tau them.)

  1. Nco ntsoov app enabled hauv Xposed Installer AllTrans-> Modules.
  2. Tua tawm AllTrans app. Nyob rau hauv "Chaw thoob ntiajteb" tab, pab kom "Siv Yandex instead of Microsoft" yog koj xav siv Yandex. Lov tes taw siv Microsoft.
  3. Xaiv qhov "Txhais los ntawm lus" thiab "Txhais los lus".
  4. Nyob rau hauv lub "Apps los txhais lus" tab, nrhiav qhov app koj xav txhais, nias tus checkbox ntawm nws. Yog tus checkbox yuav tsis pom, ua tiag rotating koj xov toj mus rau hom toj roob hauv pes.
  5. Kaw thiab Updated 10/22/07/f.Triplett app tus koj xav txhais - nws yuav tsum tau muab txhais losis siv!

Tau pes Yandex yawm sij yuav ua li cas

  1. Mus Yandex txhais API Website
  2. Yog hais tias koj tsis tau muaj tus account, nias "rau npe" thiab xee npe rau ib tus account tshiab.

Yuav ua li cas tau Microsoft Azure txhais qhov tseem ceeb

  1. Xee npe rau ib tus account Microsoft Azure.
    Yog hais tias koj tsis tau muaj tus account Azure, zwm npe rau ib tus account Microsoft Azure.
  2. Tom qab koj tau ib tug account, kos npe rau hauv qhov Azure Portal.
  3. Ntxiv ib "Microsoft lub tshuab txhais lus API Subscription" rau koj tus account Azure.
    1. Xaiv ib lub subscription "pay-as-you-go". Yuav kom tau credit card, tiam sis tsis txhob poob siab, tsis muaj dab tsi yuav tau them.
    2. Xaiv "+ tshiab" qhov.
    3. Xaiv "txoj kev ntse" los ntawm daim ntawv teev cov kev pab.
    4. Xaiv "Kev txawj ntse APIs".
    5. Xaiv "API yam" qhov.
    6. Xaiv "Txhais lus ntawv".
    7. Nyob rau hauv "Pricing Tier", xaiv qhov "dawb Tier (F0)".
    8. Sau kom tag daim ntawv, thiab nias lub pob kom "Tshwmsim".
  4. Retrieve koj "Authentication yawm sij".
    1. Mus rau qhov "Txhua Resources" thiab xaiv tau ntau Microsoft lub tshuab txhais lus API koj cuv npe rau.
    2. Mus rau qhov kev xaiv "Tuav" thiab luam koj cov subscription yuam sij rau hauv tsev, cov kev pab cuam.

TroubleShooting

Tsis app yog muab txhais ua nyob rau hauv tej app

  • Yog hais tias tsis muaj app yog muab txhais, kos yog "AllTrans" yog enabled hauv Xposed Installer-> Modules.
  • Tseem saib yog koj "Microsoft/Yandex Subscription yuam sij" ntawd puas muaj tseeb.

Txhais app nyam yog ib qho screen qhib

  • Ndliv lub npe app tus nyob rau hauv "Apps los txhais" tab, thiab nias rau ntawm "Nyem kom meej txhais lus Cache".
  • Yog hais tias qhov no yog thawj zaug koj pib txhais lus no app, nyem qhov ntawm lub npe app tus tab "Apps los txhais lus", thiab "Lwm yam chaw" sau "Qeeb ua ntej Replcaing uas txhais ua phau ntawv" 2000.

Yog tus Translated app "Force zoo"

  • Ndliv lub npe app tus nyob rau hauv "Apps los txhais" tab, thiab nias rau ntawm "Nyem kom meej txhais lus Cache".
  • Ndliv lub npe app tus nyob rau hauv "Apps los txhais lus" tab, thiab nyob rau hauv "lwm yam chaw" sau "Qeeb ua ntej Replcaing uas txhais ua phau ntawv" rau 2000.
  • Ndliv lub npe app tus nyob rau hauv "Apps los txhais" tab, thiab nyob rau hauv "Lwm yam chaw" sau "Qeeb ua ntej pib mus pes WebViews" rau 2000.

Ib qho app no tsis tau txhais

  • Ndliv lub npe app tus nyob rau hauv "Apps los txhais" tab, thiab nyob rau hauv "Lwm yam chaw" sau "Qeeb ua ntej pib mus pes WebViews" rau 2000.
  • Yog hais tias muaj qee qhov chaw hauv lub yeej tseem tsis tau txhais, nias rau lub npe app tus nyob rau hauv "Apps los txhais" tab, thiab pab kom tau "Hom txhoj puab heev".

Ib app qus no tsis tau txhais

  • Vim muaj kev, "AllTrans" yuav tsis ua hauj lwm nrog ntau yam kev ua si. Tsis muaj dab tsi yuav ua tau txog qhov no.
